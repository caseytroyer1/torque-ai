Wall Hip Drill

Stand with your back against a wall and practice your address position. Your hips, back, and head should lightly touch the wall. This builds awareness of proper spine angle and posture.

Gate Drill

Place two tees just wider than your clubhead on either side of the ball. Swing through the gate without hitting the tees. This promotes an inside-out swing path and eliminates the slice.

Short Game

Clock Chipping

Imagine a clock around the ball. Practice chips swinging to 7 o'clock (short), 8 o'clock (medium), and 9 o'clock (full). Builds consistent distance control around the greens.

One Hand Putting

Practice putting with only your lead hand, then only your trail hand. This builds feel and exposes weaknesses in your putting stroke. Do 10 putts each hand before every practice session.
